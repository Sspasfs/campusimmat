◆ serializeComponent()

serializeComponent ( Component $vObj)
protected

Gives a component a 'score' for sorting purposes.

This is solely used by the childrenSort method.

A higher score means the item will be lower in the list. To avoid score collisions, each "score category" has a reasonable space to accommodate elements. The $key is added to the $score to preserve the original relative order of elements.
