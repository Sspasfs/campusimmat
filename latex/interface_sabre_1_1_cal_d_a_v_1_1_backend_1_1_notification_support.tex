\doxysubsection{Description détaillée}
Adds caldav notification support to a backend.

Note\+: This feature is experimental, and may change in between different Sabre\+DAV versions.

Notifications are defined at\+: \href{http://svn.calendarserver.org/repository/calendarserver/CalendarServer/trunk/doc/Extensions/caldav-notifications.txt}{\texttt{ http\+://svn.\+calendarserver.\+org/repository/calendarserver/\+Calendar\+Server/trunk/doc/\+Extensions/caldav-\/notifications.\+txt}}

These notifications are basically a list of server-\/generated notifications displayed to the user. Users can dismiss notifications by deleting them.

The primary usecase is to allow for calendar-\/sharing.
