\doxysubsection{Description détaillée}
\doxylink{class_v_object}{VObject} splitter.

The splitter is responsible for reading a large v\+Card or i\+Calendar object, and splitting it into multiple objects.

This is for example for Card and Cal\+DAV, which require every event and vcard to exist in their own objects, instead of one large one.
