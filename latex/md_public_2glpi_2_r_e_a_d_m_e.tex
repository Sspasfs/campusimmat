\doxylink{class_g_l_p_i}{GLPI} stands for {\bfseries{Gestionnaire Libre de Parc Informatique}} is a Free Asset and IT Management \doxylink{class_software}{Software} package, that provides ITIL Service Desk features, licenses tracking and software auditing.

Major \doxylink{class_g_l_p_i}{GLPI} Features\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Service Asset and Configuration Management (SACM)}}\+: Manages your IT assets and configurations, tracks computers, peripherals, network printers, and their associated components. With native dynamic inventory management from version 10 onwards, you can maintain an up-\/to-\/date configuration database, ensuring accurate and timely information about your assets.
\item {\bfseries{Request Fulfillment}}\+: Streamlines request fulfillment processes, making it easy to manage service requests, incidents, and problems efficiently. This ensures that user requests are handled promptly and professionally, enhancing overall service quality.
\item {\bfseries{Incident and \doxylink{class_problem}{Problem} Management}}\+: Supports efficient handling of ITIL\textquotesingle{}s Incident Management and \doxylink{class_problem}{Problem} Management processes. Ensures that issues are addressed promptly, root causes are identified, and preventive measures are taken.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_change}{Change} Management}}\+: Supports change management processes, enabling you to plan, review, and implement changes in a controlled and standardized manner. This helps minimize disruptions and risks associated with changes to your IT environment.
\item {\bfseries{Knowledge Management}}\+: Includes a knowledge base and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) support, facilitating knowledge management. Allows you to capture, store, and share valuable information and solutions, empowering your team to resolve issues more effectively.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_contract}{Contract} Management}}\+: Offers comprehensive contract management capabilities, including managing contracts, contacts, and associated documents related to inventory items. Aligns with ITIL\textquotesingle{}s \doxylink{class_supplier}{Supplier} Management process, ensuring you have control and visibility over your contracts and vendor relationships.
\item {\bfseries{Financial Management for IT Services}}\+: Assists in managing financial information, such as purchase orders, warranty details, and depreciation. Aligns with ITIL\textquotesingle{}s Financial Management for IT Services process, helping you optimize IT spending and investments.
\item {\bfseries{Asset \doxylink{class_reservation}{Reservation}}}\+: Offers asset reservation functionality, allowing you to reserve IT assets for specific purposes or periods. Aligns with ITIL\textquotesingle{}s Demand Management process, ensuring resources are allocated effectively based on demand.
\item {\bfseries{Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM)}}\+: Provides features for managing data center infrastructure, enhancing control over critical assets.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_software}{Software} and License Management}}\+: Includes functionality for managing software and licenses, ensuring compliance and cost control.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_impact}{Impact} Analysis}}\+: Supports impact analysis, helping assess the potential consequences of changes or incidents on IT services.
\item {\bfseries{Service Catalog (with \doxylink{class_s_l_m}{SLM})}}\+: Includes service catalog features, often linked with Service Level Management (\doxylink{class_s_l_m}{SLM}), to define and manage available services.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_entity}{Entity} Separation}}\+: Offers entity separation features, allowing distinct management of different organizational units or entities.
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_project}{Project} Management}}\+: Supports project management, helping organize and track projects and associated tasks.
\item {\bfseries{Intervention \doxylink{class_planning}{Planning}}}\+: Offers intervention planning capabilities for scheduling and managing on-\/site interventions.
\end{DoxyItemize}

\doxylink{class_g_l_p_i}{GLPI} is a living software. Improvements are continuously made, new functionalities are being developed, and issues are being fixed.

To ease support and development, we need your help when encountering issues. There is a \doxylink{class_g_l_p_i}{GLPI} version typical lifecycle\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item A new major version (9.\+3) is released.
\item Minor versions (9.\+3.\+x), fixing bugs or issues, are published after several weeks. Please consider updating to the latest released minor version if you encounter some bugs or performance issues.
\item Several months after major version released, a new major version (9.\+4) is released. Previous major versions become unsupported, please update to the new major version. Obviously, we provide support for the migration tools too! 
\end{DoxyItemize}
