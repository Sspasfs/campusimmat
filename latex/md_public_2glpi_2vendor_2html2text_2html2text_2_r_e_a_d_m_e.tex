This library started life on the blog of Jon Abernathy \href{http://www.chuggnutt.com/html2text}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+chuggnutt.\+com/html2text}}

A number of projects picked up the library and started using it -\/ among those was Round\+Cube mail. They made a number of updates to it over time to suit their webmail client.

Now it has been extracted as a standalone library. Hopefully it can be of use to others.
