{\ttfamily Laminas\textbackslash{}Mime} is a support class for handling multipart MIME messages. It is used by {\ttfamily Laminas\textbackslash{}Mail} and {\ttfamily Laminas\textbackslash{}Mime\textbackslash{}Message} and may be used by applications requiring MIME support.\hypertarget{md_public_2glpi_2vendor_2laminas_2laminas-mime_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md6285}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Installation}{Installation}}\label{md_public_2glpi_2vendor_2laminas_2laminas-mime_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md6285}
